The folder represents the foundation for a replication exercise to be conducted that A repeats (checks) the findings of the original paper, critically evaluates the assumptions, limitations, exclusion criteria, identification stratagy and further of the paper. 
B. We extend the paper by provided an expansion of the original paper to improve it
Structure: 
Input contains the DTA files for the outputs of the original paper
For each DTA file, we aim to put our outputs in the output file. 
To work with the data in the inputs, read the READ ME file.

Criteria: 
Excellent Rating - follow the structure below rigorously.
Understanding of the paper
Clearly explains the research question, historical context, and main contribution; convincingly discusses why the relationship may be causal
Explanation of the empirical strategy
Clearly explains identification strategy, data sources, variable construction, estimation logic, and key threats to identification (incl. role of IV)
Replication of core results
Successfully replicates main results, presents them clearly, covers key baseline specifications, and explains any discrepancies
Extension of the analysis
Provides a well-motivated, relevant extension that engages directly with the core argument and adds meaningful analytical value
Structure, writing, and use of evidence
Well organized, clearly written, uses evidence effectively, and presents tables/figures and citations professionally

It is also important to keep an academic, PhD tone and level. 

See the Replications 1 Feedback to make sure we keep up with their ideals.
